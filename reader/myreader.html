My Reader

This is a reader in a very general sense. Just an app that the user can use ton consumer content published somewhere else. It register

The reader application is able to register by including an iframe with the src element pointing to http://subtome.com/register.html?name=(Reader Name)&handler=(Subscription handler url)>

The nameis a free form string. (It will be rendered as a string, so HTML will be escaped).

The handler param is an absolute URL to the page used in the reader to perform subscriptions. Do not forget to add the {url} placeholder in your urls where the url that the user wants to subscribe to will be added.
